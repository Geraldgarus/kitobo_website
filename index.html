About Us

A Sanctuary of Serenity & Comfort

Kitobo Serenity Resort is a luxury boutique retreat nestled in the heart of Arusha, Tanzania — offering breathtaking views of Mount Meru, lush natural surroundings, and world-class hospitality.

Whether you're here for business or leisure, our resort provides an unmatched blend of comfort, elegance, and authentic Tanzanian warmth. From our beautifully appointed rooms to our vibrant bar and restaurant, every detail is crafted to exceed your expectations.
