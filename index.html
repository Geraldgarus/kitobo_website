About Us

A Sanctuary of Serenity & Comfort

Kitobo Serenity Resort is a luxury boutique retreat in the heart of Arusha, Tanzania — offering breathtaking Mount Meru views, lush surroundings, and world-class hospitality.

From beautifully appointed rooms to our vibrant bar and restaurant, every detail is crafted to exceed expectations. Whether here for business or leisure, experience unmatched comfort and authentic Tanzanian warmth.
