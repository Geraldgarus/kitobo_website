A Feast for the Senses

At Kitobo Serenity Resort, dining is an experience. Our restaurant and bar blend the finest local ingredients with international techniques to deliver unforgettable flavours in a serene, natural setting.

The Restaurant

Farm-to-Table Excellence

Our restaurant celebrates the incredible produce of Tanzania. Each menu is crafted with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, combining East African culinary traditions with modern international flair.

From hearty breakfasts as the morning mist lifts over Mount Meru, to elegantly prepared dinners by candlelight — every meal at Kitobo tells a delicious story.

Our bar is the heart of Kitobo's social scene. Whether you prefer a classic cocktail, a local Tanzanian brew, or a carefully selected wine, our bartenders craft each drink with skill and passion.
