Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes! We arrange airport transfers upon request. Please contact us at least 24 hours before arrival so we can prepare a comfortable pickup for you.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Yes, a hearty breakfast featuring local and continental options is included for all guests every morning from 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM.

Do you assist with safari arrangements?

Absolutely. Our concierge team has strong partnerships with top safari operators in the Arusha region and can arrange day trips or extended safaris.

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be arranged subject to availability.

Is there free parking at the resort?

Yes, we offer complimentary secure parking for all resident guests throughout your stay.

Can I make a dining reservation without staying?

Yes! Our restaurant and bar welcome non-resident guests. Please call us or use the contact form above to reserve your table.
