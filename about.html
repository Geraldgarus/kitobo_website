Who We Are

A Retreat Born from a Love of Tanzania

Kitobo Serenity Resort was founded with a single vision — to create a space where guests could disconnect from the rush of life and reconnect with the beauty of nature. Situated at the foot of the majestic Mount Meru in Arusha, Tanzania, we are more than a resort; we are a sanctuary.

Our founders, deeply inspired by the warmth of Tanzanian culture and the stunning natural landscape, set out to build a place that honours both. Every brick, every garden path, every carefully chosen interior element tells a story of passion, craft, and dedication.

Today, Kitobo Serenity Resort stands as a testament to that vision — a boutique luxury destination where world-class comfort meets the authentic soul of East Africa.

The Experience

Every Stay is a Memory

From the moment you arrive, Kitobo Serenity Resort envelops you in an atmosphere of calm, elegance, and natural beauty. Wake up to birdsong and mountain views. Savour fresh coffee on your private terrace as mist lifts from Mount Meru.

Spend your days exploring Arusha's incredible wildlife reserves, relaxing in our serene environment, or connecting with colleagues in our business-friendly spaces. As evening falls, let our bar and restaurant transport you with exceptional food and handcrafted cocktails.
