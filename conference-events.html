Where Business Meets Serenity

Kitobo Serenity Resort offers a unique and inspiring setting for conferences, corporate meetings, workshops, and special events. Nestled at the foot of Mount Meru, our venue combines professional facilities with the calming beauty of nature.

Whether you are planning an intimate boardroom meeting or a large-scale corporate event, our dedicated team will ensure every detail is handled with precision and warmth.

Let the peaceful surroundings of Arusha inspire creativity, focus, and connection among your team or guests.
